Site Plan: Homerix Real Estate

1. General Description

Site Name: Homerix Real Estate

Purpose: The primary purpose of the Homerix website is to serve as a high-end digital storefront for a modern real estate agency. It aims to attract potential property buyers and sellers by showcasing premium listings in an engaging and visually appealing manner. The site provides an immersive experience to discover properties and an easy way to make contact.

Target Audience: The target audience includes prospective homebuyers, property investors, and individuals looking to sell their properties. The sleek, dark theme and professional imagery target a clientele interested in modern, high-value real estate.

3. Site Structure

The site is a cohesive three-page experience designed to guide users from discovery to contact. The structure is as follows:

  • Header: A consistent, sticky header appears on all pages, featuring the Homerix logo and primary navigation (Home, Discover, Contact). It includes a responsive hamburger menu for mobile.
  • Homepage: Serves as an engaging entry point with a full-screen hero slider, a grid of "New Arrival" properties, and cards highlighting the company's "Core Strengths."
  • Discover Page: An immersive, full-screen carousel experience allowing users to browse featured properties in detail, with an interactive "See More" view.
  • Contact Page: A comprehensive contact hub with a submission form, direct contact details, an image grid for visual engagement, and a client testimonial slider.
  • Footer: A consistent footer on all pages provides essential links, company information, and copyright details.
